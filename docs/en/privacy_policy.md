# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 20, 2026

ATBCmder is built with your privacy as our top priority. We believe that your files, data, and usage habits belong exclusively to you and should remain entirely under your control.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information or user data.** 

ATBCmder operates entirely locally on your macOS device. 
- It does not "phone home" to our servers.
- It does not include any third-party analytics trackers, telemetry, or crash reporters.
- It does not harvest, scan, or analyze your file metadata, browsing habits, or personal information.

## Network Access
Any network connections made by ATBCmder (such as FTP, SFTP, WebDAV, or Samba connections) are established directly between your device and the remote servers you explicitly configure. We do not proxy, intercept, monitor, or have access to these connections or the data transmitted through them.

## App Store Guidelines
This policy is provided to clarify our data practices in accordance with the Apple App Store guidelines. Because we collect absolutely zero data from our users, there is no data to share, sell, or delete.
